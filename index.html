Abstract

Audio-driven human animation methods have shown strong performance in synchronized facial motion generation. However, most prior works focus on single-person settings and struggle with multi-stream audio and instruction following. We introduce a new task and a framework that addresses multi-person conversational video generation with better person-audio binding and controllability.
